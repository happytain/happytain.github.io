15.6.20Intro

Ça se passe dans un petit appartement. Il donne sur un fleuve puis sur la mer d’un coté, de l’autre sur le bout de la ville. Est proche de l’ancienne gare qui n’est plus utilisée et d'une école. Beaucoup de constructions dans les alentours. Des appartements et des maisons de nouveaux riches. Un parc et beaucoup d’arbres. Puis les vieux appartements des gens qui habitaient là, à côté des trains, avant qu’ils ne cessent de passer.

8.6.20Note: Things to remember

- Meaningful interactions between the game as a simulation and the player as an active and evolving participant.
- The player is entity that is driven, consciously or subconsciously, to learn new skills high in perceived value. They gain pleasure from successfully acquiring skills.
- A skill is a behavior that the player uses to manipulate the world. Some skills are conceptual, such as navigating a map while others are quite physical, such as pounding in a nail with a hammer.
- It feels good to “get” it.

What is this

This blog is about games mostly. I'd like to upload my thoughts about games I play or research. I think it could also be a good diary for the WIP of a click-and-click menu driven adventure game—some of a visual novel I don't have a name for yet. In this game you decide where two characters, Heliante (H) and Joelle (J) move around a small city apartment. They interact with each other and the space. Each decision moves them towards one of four endings. I like to write in French and English according to my mood. Send me a msg via "contact" if you want to chat.
