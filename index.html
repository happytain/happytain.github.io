6.15.20Intro (feb '20)

Ça se passe dans un petit appartement. Il donne sur un fleuve puis sur la mer d’un coté, de l’autre sur le bout de la ville. Est proche de l’ancienne gare qui n’est plus utilisée et d'une école. 6.8.20Note: Things to remember

- Meaningful interactions between the game as a simulation and the player as an active and evolving participant.
- The player is entity that is driven, consciously or subconsciously, to learn new skills high in perceived value. They gain pleasure from successfully acquiring skills.
- A skill is a behavior that the player uses to manipulate the world. Some skills are conceptual, such as navigating a map while others are quite physical, such as pounding in a nail with a hammer.
- It feels good to “get” it.

? What's this ?

This blog details the WIP of a click-and-click menu driven adventure game—some kind of visual novel. I don't have a name for it yet. But in this game you decide where two characters, Heliante (H) and Joelle (J) move around a small city apartment. They interact with each other and the space; if they run into something, you guide them through the interaction. Each decision moves them towards one of four ends. This game will take me three years to make. I write in French and English according to my mood!
